About LuxeLayer

Welcome to LuxeLayer – a fashion brand under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD. LuxeLayer represents style, confidence, and luxury at affordable prices.

Our journey is an inspiration – we achieved 1 Crore+ revenue before the age of 18, proving that passion, vision, and hard work can break every limit.

Every outfit we create reflects our belief: "Clothing is not just fashion, it’s a lifestyle."

Alok Kumar

Founder & CEO of LuxeLayer | Visionary Entrepreneur

At just 17 years old, Alok Kumar built assets worth crores and founded LuxeLayer – a brand that has already crossed ₹1 Crore+ revenue before turning 18. Under his leadership, LuxeLayer is proudly part of SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD.

Our Story

2022 - The Beginning

Alok Kumar started trading & entrepreneurship journey as a teenager.

2023 - Idea of LuxeLayer

The idea of building a youth-focused clothing brand was born.

2024 - 1 Crore+ Milestone

LuxeLayer achieved 1 Crore+ revenue before Alok turned 18.

2025 - Growth & Expansion

LuxeLayer became a unit of SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD. aiming for global reach.
