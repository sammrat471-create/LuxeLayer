Alok Kumar

Born 4 March 2008 · Student · Trader · Entrepreneur

Alok Kumar is the founder of LuxeLayer, a contemporary fashion brand operating under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD. The concept for LuxeLayer originated in 2023, operations were initiated in 2024, and the brand officially launched in 2025. Driven by vision and persistence, Alok built the company while still a student and reached a major personal milestone — earning over one crore before turning 18.

LuxeLayer is committed to delivering premium, trend-forward clothing while creating measurable social impact. Alok has pledged that the company's revenue will be directed toward public-benefit initiatives — primarily in education and healthcare — to ensure the brand's success benefits the wider community.
