Alok Kumar

Founder & CEO, LuxeLayer
Under Sammrat Group Pvt. Ltd.

Alok Kumar (born on 4th March 2008) is a young student, trader, and entrepreneur who founded LuxeLayer under Sammrat Group Pvt. Ltd. At just 17 years of age, he has achieved milestones worth crores, proving that determination and vision know no age. The idea of LuxeLayer came in 2023, started in 2024, and was officially established in 2025.

With a mission to give back, Alok has pledged that all company revenue will be donated for the benefit of society, especially in the fields of education and healthcare. His journey reflects resilience, innovation, and a vision for a better future.
