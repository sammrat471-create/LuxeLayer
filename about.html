About LuxeLayer

Welcome to LuxeLayer – a fashion brand under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD. LuxeLayer represents style, confidence, and luxury at affordable prices.

Our Vision

To become a global fashion powerhouse under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD., leading the new era of luxury streetwear.
