About LuxeLayer

LuxeLayer is a premium clothing brand under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD.

Founded by Alok Kumar, LuxeLayer redefines fashion with a vision to make luxury accessible to everyone.

By the age of just 18, LuxeLayer had already crossed 1 Crore+ revenue milestone, setting a new benchmark in youth entrepreneurship.

Founder’s Story

Alok Kumar, a young visionary and entrepreneur, laid the foundation of LuxeLayer even before turning 18. With determination, creativity, and passion for fashion, he built a brand that crossed 1 Crore+ revenue at a record-breaking age.

Growing up with a dream to blend luxury and accessibility, Alok turned his vision into reality by launching LuxeLayer under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD.

His journey is an inspiration for the youth, proving that age is never a barrier when you have courage, consistency, and creativity. Today, LuxeLayer is not just a brand — it’s a movement to empower young dreamers.
