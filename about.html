About LuxeLayer

LuxeLayer is a premium clothing brand under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD.

Founded by Alok Kumar, LuxeLayer redefines fashion with a vision to make luxury accessible to everyone. By the age of just 18, LuxeLayer had already crossed 1 Crore+ revenue milestone, setting a new benchmark in youth entrepreneurship.

Our mission is to provide stylish, high-quality, and comfortable clothing that inspires confidence and elegance in every individual.
