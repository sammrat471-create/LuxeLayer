About the Founder

Alok Kumar, born on 4th March 2008, is the young and dynamic founder of LuxeLayer, a brand operating under SAMMRAT GROUP.PVT.LTD.

A student, trader, and entrepreneur, Alok had the vision of creating this company in 2023. He began the journey in 2024, and by 2025 LuxeLayer became a reality. At just 17 years of age, he achieved extraordinary success, earning more than crores through his ventures.

With a deep sense of responsibility, Alok has pledged that all company revenue will be donated towards causes like education and health, empowering communities and creating lasting impact.
