The Story of LuxeLayer

A premium fashion and lifestyle brand proudly established under SAMMRAT GROUP PVT. LTD. Born from an idea in 2023, meticulously crafted through 2024, and brought to life in 2025 to redefine the boundaries of luxury, comfort, and accessibility.


Alok Sammrat

Founder & CEO | Born: 4 March 2008

Behind the elegance of LuxeLayer stands a visionary who defied the conventional timelines of success. Alok Sammrat is a prodigy entrepreneur, an astute trader, and a dedicated student who achieved total financial independence at the remarkable age of 17.

Generating revenue in crores through strategic trading and business acumen, his journey is a masterclass in hard work, unyielding dedication, and the profound belief that age is never a barrier to monumental success.

At LuxeLayer, he believes fashion is an extension of identity. Every collection under his direction is engineered with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring top-tier fabrics, architectural designs, and an unmatched sensory experience.

“My mission is not just to build a successful company, but to create an enduring impact. 100% of the revenue generated by LuxeLayer is dedicated to helping people in need—specifically advancing healthcare and education.”

— Alok Sammrat

Our Mission

Bridging the gap between high-end aesthetics and everyday accessibility.

  • Deliver unique, premium fashion accessible to everyone.
  • Harmonize style, extreme comfort, and innovation in every thread.
  • Fund and support society through dedicated donations to healthcare and education.

The Future

Our roadmap to global recognition while staying rooted in our core values.

  • Establish exclusive LuxeLayer flagship outlets across India.
  • Architect a flawless, immersive online fashion experience.
  • Elevate LuxeLayer into a globally trusted and recognized empire.
