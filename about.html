Alok Kumar

Founder & CEO, LuxeLayer
Under Sammrat Group Pvt. Ltd.

Alok Kumar (born 4 March 2008) is a student, trader, and entrepreneur who founded LuxeLayer under Sammrat Group Pvt. Ltd. The idea for LuxeLayer was conceived in 2023, operations began in 2024, and the brand was officially established in 2025. By age 17, Alok has reached remarkable financial milestones and built a business guided by purpose — pledging that company revenue will be directed to social causes, with a strong focus on education and healthcare.

Passionate about responsible growth and community impact, Alok leads LuxeLayer with integrity, innovation, and a commitment to use success to uplift others.
